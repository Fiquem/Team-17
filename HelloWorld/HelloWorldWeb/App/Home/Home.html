    How this app works

    This app works as a guide while writing a essay. It will tell you if you are near complete to your required word count. If you have left out references or keywords and it will tick them off automatically when included.

    First time using app

    Choose a template and will display required functions for that topic. Fill out each function as described below.

    Assignment Progress

    This is the overall progress of your document.

    Word Count & Margin Percentage

    The amount of words required for your document, and the margin percentage will calculate your min and max count. For example 100 words with margin of 10% will return min 90 and max 110. Progress bar will turn red when actual word count goes over required count.

    Min Count & Max Count

    The range where you want your word count to fall in between when near finished.

    Structure & Subpoints

    What is required or what you want in your document. Tick them off when included, press enter to add new subpoint.

    References & Key Words

    Will check if you have included your references or key words in your document.

    Books

    Books you used in your essay, when finished tick the required box.

    Useful Links

    While working on a assignment you can save links, that you will need again for easy access. Just copy and paste url into text box and press enter.

    Why dont I see any changes in progress bar

  • Check if word count/ margin are filled out appropriate .
  • Make sure all functions are filled out and ticked off if needed.
